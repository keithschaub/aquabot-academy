Your Build Journey

1Initial Assembly

4Brain Installation

Mount the Arduino R4 WiFi board - the intelligent brain that will control your boat.

5Control Wiring

6Programming

Program the AI control system and implement remote operation capabilities.

7Waterproofing

Apply waterproofing techniques to protect your boat's electronic components.
